Who we are

A dynamic training and consulting firm, built to close the skills gap.

IFLADS is a dynamic training and consulting firm dedicated to bridging knowledge gaps and empowering professionals with future-ready skills. We work with individuals, professionals, and organizations across tech consultancy, data analytics, qualitative research, and career development.

  • Hands-on training across Power BI, Excel, SQL, R, Python, automation, AI, and data governance
  • Qualitative research support using NVivo and ATLAS.ti for academic, nonprofit, and public health work
  • Community learning and mentorship initiatives for early-career and aspiring data professionals
